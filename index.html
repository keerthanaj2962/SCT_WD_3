Tic Tac Toe

Rules for Tic-Tac-toe

1.The game is played on a grid that's 3 squares by 3 squares.
2.You are X, your friend is 0.Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.
3.The first player to get 3 of her marks in a row(up,down,across or diagonally)is te winner.
4.when all 9 squares are full, the game is over.if no player has 3 marks in a row,the game ends in a tie.
